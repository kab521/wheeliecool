About Wheelie Cool

Creative Team

Kathryn Bager

Site Editor & Founder

Kathryn created Wheelie Cool to make motorcycle culture feel more approachable to beginner riders and students. She combines editorial writing, web design, and rider research to build a space focused on accessibility, creativity, and honest reviews.

Jordan Smith

Performance Analyst

Jordan researches motorcycle specifications and performance trends to help new riders understand how different bikes actually behave on the road.

Maya Torres

Gear Specialist

Maya reviews helmets, jackets, gloves, and rider accessories with a focus on affordability and safety for beginner riders.

Sophia Patel

Campus Safety Contributor

Sophia contributes rider safety guides and local awareness resources focused on college students and urban riders.

Our Story

2026 — The Idea

Wheelie Cool started as a class project inspired by motorcycle culture on college campuses and social media.

2026 — Building the First Reviews

Early articles focused on beginner sportbikes, rider terminology, and honest first impressions.

2026 — Expanding the Community

Wheelie Cool expanded into gear guides, safety resources, rider maps, and multimedia content.

Editorial Mission

Wheelie Cool blends motorcycle journalism, student culture, and visual storytelling into a beginner-friendly digital magazine.
