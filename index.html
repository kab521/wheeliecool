Meant For Wheelie Cool Beginners

This website is for beginners who are curious about motorcycles but may feel nervous or overwhelmed by the community. As someone learning myself, I wanted to create a space that’s not intense, but easy to understand. Wheelie Cool is a place where users can learn alongside me through simple explanations, personal experiences, and beginner-friendly resources. Here, you can explore the differences between bikes, read reviews, view safe riding zones around Rutgers University, and discover real data and narratives about the riding experience. The goal of this site is not to act like an expert guide, but to make motorcycles feel less intimidating and more approachable for anyone interested in learning at their own pace.
